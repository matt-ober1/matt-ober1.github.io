About Me

This knowledge is complemented by hands-on experience gained through lab exercises and practical projects, which have sharpened my ability to analyze and mitigate security threats effectively. While it may not be required for the Google Course, I am using this website as a way to challenge myself as web application development is something that I have struggled with.
